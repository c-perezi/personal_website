# Teamwork and Collaboration

## Group Work Experience

Group J was fairly diverse, composed of graduate and undergraduate students. This provided for a varied experience across the spectrum, which was a useful learning experience. 

---

## Key Lessons Learned

The main lesson learned as part of this teamwork experience was that communication, soft skills, and the ability to move together as a team is highly important academically and in the workplace. 

---

## Challenges and How We Navigated Them

Technical gaps, such as younger group members not having as much experience with Python, were addressed by mentoring them and explaining portions of the code. Other gaps were around scheduling in an online class, as this added a high level of complexity. We learned to use scheduling tools as part of this process.
