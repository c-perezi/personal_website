## Course Outcomes

### Outcome 1
Recognize when a problem is not amenable to a traditional programming (e.g.,
procedural, object-oriented, etc.) solution but might be amenable to informed or
uninformed search with or without constraints, knowledge-based or learningbased methods.

### Outcome 2
Explain and apply AI algorithms in diverse problem types.

### Outcome 3
Analyze problems by comparing different AI methods to decide the most appropriate
approach or approaches for their solution.

### Outcome 4
Design and implement an AI system.
